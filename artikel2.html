Which BPJS Health and Health Insurance Is Better?


Provision of health services through BPJS Health is a manifestation of State responsibility in providing social security for citizens. Since its inception in 2014 until now, BPJS Health has been instrumental in improving the quality of public health. However, is this service really giving satisfaction to everyone?

Although providing services to anyone, does not necessarily make everyone feel comfortable and fit to use the service BPJS Health compared with insurance. Government policies that require all workers to use BPJS Health services become the reason companies stop using other health insurance that has been used. Paying two premiums at a time is considered to burden the company's expenses.

However BPJS Health is different from the insurance previously used. The various shortcomings and difficulties when using BPJS Health facilities should be considered. Because every person would want to get adequate health insurance.

If you look at this fact, choosing other insurance services is a wise decision, especially for some employees who do have sufficient income to buy insurance. There is no harm in using personal health insurance services as long as the insurance can provide maximum benefits.

Intend to take out insurance? Two choices of insurance products below you should consider.

1. Link Unit Health Insurance

Unit link health insurance can be said is now in demand many people. Because this insurance offers two advantages: self protection and investment. Thus, the premium paid by the customer will be placed in two allocations, namely for insurance protection and investment to some investment instruments.

As with any other type of investment, link units also have a number of risks. This is not well understood by customers. The customer does not know that the paid premium may be reduced due to unfavorable capital market situation. So, understand these risks if you want to buy unit link.

Watch The Following Points Before Buying Unit Link
It is important to understand in detail about unit link insurance. At least, some points below can be used as consideration.

1. Number of Premiums Paid Greater

The premium to be paid by a unit-linked insurance customer will be far greater than pure health insurance. Because some of these funds will be allocated not only for self-protection as well as investment.

2. Limited Health Benefits

Because it serves as a self-protection and investment, unit link is still under pure health insurance in providing benefits. In premium returns, unit link insurance is better. Premiums paid by customers will not be forfeited and can be withdrawn if at any time they need it.

2. Pure Health Insurance

With other link units offering investment benefits, pure health insurance only focuses on providing health services. As previously mentioned, in the provision of health services, pure health insurance is superior to unit link.

Points in Proper Poor Health Insurance Considered
Between unit link insurance and pure health there are differences, both from the advantages and disadvantages. The following are the points on the advantages of pure health insurance that deserve consideration.

1. Adequate Protection Guarantee

Pure health insurance has excellent service. For outpatient or dental care, pure health insurance provides dependents, while link units do not provide these dependents. In addition, the term of his policy in a matter of years. You are free in deciding whether to extend or not.

2. Wider Health Services

Pure health insurance also has a wide and comprehensive range of health services. It is not owned by unit link insurance.

3. Low Premium

This insurance also offers a mild premium because there is no investment benefit in it. This allows pure health insurance to provide excellent health services to its customers.
